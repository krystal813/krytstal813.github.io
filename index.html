Brandwein's Bagels

A family-owned bagel shop in Chapel Hill

Brandwein's Bagels has been serving delicious, freshly baked bagels in Chapel Hill for over 20 years. Their bagels are made with the finest ingredients and baked to perfection every day. They offer a variety of flavors, including plain, everything, cinnamon raisin, and more. Their bagels are perfect for breakfast, lunch, or any time of day.

Stop by their shop today and try one of their delicious bagels. They also offer coffee, tea, and other breakfast items!
